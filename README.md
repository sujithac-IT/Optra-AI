# Optra AI: An AI-Powered Career Operating System

## Overview

Optra AI is an intelligent career platform designed to help students discover internships, jobs, hackathons, scholarships, and learning opportunities through personalized AI recommendations. The platform simplifies career planning by bringing multiple resources into a single, unified experience.

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to find relevant career opportunities because information is spread across multiple platforms. This leads to missed deadlines, limited visibility into opportunities, and inefficient career planning.

## Solution

Optra AI uses Google's AI technologies to analyze a student's profile, skills, and interests to provide:

- Personalized career recommendations
- Resume analysis and ATS insights
- Internship and job discovery
- Hackathon and scholarship recommendations
- Customized learning roadmaps
